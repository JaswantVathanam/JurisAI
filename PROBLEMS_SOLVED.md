## 📋 Executive Summary

The **AI Legal Assistant** is a comprehensive legal technology platform designed to address critical inefficiencies in India's justice delivery system. With **5+ crore pending cases**, **2-3 month average FIR processing times**, and **80% of citizens unable to afford legal representation**, India's judicial system faces unprecedented challenges.

This software directly addresses these challenges by leveraging artificial intelligence, multi-language support, and role-based workflows to democratize access to justice for **1.4 billion Indians**.
